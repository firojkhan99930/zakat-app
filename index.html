Zakat Kis Maal Par?

4 main categories

🥇

Sona (Gold)

7.5 tola (87.48g) ya zyada gold ho aur 1 saal guzar gaya ho.

🥈

Chandi (Silver)

52.5 tola (612.36g) ya zyada chandi ho aur 1 saal guzar gaya ho.

💵

Cash / Bank / Udhar

Ghar ka cash + bank balance + jo udhar diya ho (wapas milne ki umeed ho) — sab milake 52.5 tola chandi ki value ke barabar ya zyada ho.

🏠

Property / Vehicle / Business

Sirf wo property/vehicle jo sale ya munafe ke liye rakhi ho. Rehne wala ghar aur personal gaadi par zakat nahi lagti — inhe chhhod do.

📐

Zakat Ka Formula

Kul Maal × 2.5%
= Tumhari Zakat
1
Sab jodo: Gold + Chandi + Cash + Bank + Udhar + Business Stock
2
Qarz minus karo: Jo tumhe kisi ko dena hai
3
Nisab check karo: Kya total 52.5 tola chandi ki value se zyada hai?
4
2.5% nikalo: Yahi tumhari Zakat hai
💡

Practical Examples

❌ Example 1

Kisi ke paas sirf 6 tola gold hai — aur kuch nahi, na cash na chandi.

🚫 Zakat NAHI lagegi — Gold nisab (7.5 tola) se kam hai.

✅ Example 2

Kisi ke paas 1 tola gold + 52.5 tola chandi ki value ka cash/bank/udhar bhi hai.

✅ Zakat LAGEGI — Gold + Cash milake nisab poora ho gaya. Poore maal par 2.5% nikalna hoga.

✅ Example 3

Kisi ke paas sirf cash/bank hai — gold/chandi kuch nahi — lekin amount 52.5 tola chandi ki value se zyada hai.

✅ Zakat LAGEGI — Sirf cash par bhi lagti hai agar nisab poora ho.

Zaroori Baatein

📅

Haul (1 Saal)

Nisab ke barabar maal par poora ek lunar/Hijri saal guzarna zaroori hai.

💍

Pehni Hui Jewellery

Ulema mein ikhtilaaf hai — احتياط (caution) ke liye nikalna behtar hai.

🏠

Rehne Wala Ghar

Apne rehne ka ghar aur daily use ki gaadi — in par zakat nahi lagti, inhe count mat karo.

🕌

Mufti Se Poochho
